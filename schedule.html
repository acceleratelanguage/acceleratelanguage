Tutoring


Language tutoring is designed to help you learn your target language by providing you with a specialized language instructor. Language tutoring services are currently being offered in English (all levels), Spanish (all levels), Portuguese (all levels). Ancient Greet (Attic, Homeric, and Koiné - introductory through advanced levels) and Latin (introductory level). If you have any questions about language tutoring, submit your inquiry here.


Coaching


Language coaching is intended to help you learn your target language by providing you with a language-learning expert. Since language coaching is more about applying generalized language learning skills than it is specialized language tutoring, we offer coaching for whatever your target language may be. If you have any questions about language coaching, submit your inquiry here.
